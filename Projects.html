Project: Gaoli Beauty

Gaoli Beauty: A Vision of Modern Elegance

One of the projects I'm most proud of is Gaoli Beauty, a brand concept I created as part of my entrepreneurship and marketing activities. Gaoli Beauty allowed me to explore the full creative process behind a beauty brand from choosing the name and designing the aesthetic to thinking about the target audience, values, and product ideas. This project helped me understand how branding works in the real world and showed me how much I enjoy connecting creativity with strategy. It also strengthened my interest in building my own beauty brand in the future and gave me confidence to keep developing my skills in marketing and design.
