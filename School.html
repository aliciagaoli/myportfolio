My School Experience & Projects

Throughout high school, I've worked on several projects that helped shape my interests and skills. I've created flowcharts, visual activities, podcast scripts, poster presentations, and branding-style assignments that allowed me to use creativity and build clear communication. I've also researched career paths, university programs, entrepreneurship ideas, and marketing strategies, which helped me understand the kind of professional I want to become. One of my favorite parts of school projects is designing visuals and developing concepts — whether it's a brand idea, a social science reflection, or a marketing plan. These experiences taught me how to organize information, think creatively, solve problems, and present ideas in a more professional way. They also motivated me to focus on improving my confidence and communication so I can express myself better in future academic and career opportunities.
